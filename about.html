Hiya, I’m Inanna!
You can also call me Kola. I’m currently completing my BFA in Design and Technology at Parsons School of Design in Manhattan. I enjoy finding new ways to animate the inanimate, often finding myself attached to my own creations. My inspiration is pulled from living sources and the digital and tangible world around me. At the moment, my work is split between interactive digital storytelling and multimedia animation. In a perfect world, I’d slip seamlessly from project to project while maintaining enough income to avoid an impending future of couch-surfing.
